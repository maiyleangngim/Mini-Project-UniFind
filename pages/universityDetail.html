Institution Overview

The Royal University of Phnom Penh (RUPP) is one of the oldest and most respected public universities in Cambodia. It plays a major role in higher education, research, and training professionals across many fields.

RUPP is a top choice for Cambodian students who want a quality, affordable education, especially in fields like IT, languages, and social sciences.

Accreditation

Fully accredited by the Ministry of Education, Youth and Sport of Cambodia.
